Responsive Grid

This content will span 10 out of 12 columns when your window has a "medium" or "large" width. In other words, this content will span 10 out of 12 columns if your window is:

  • Medium—meaning greater than 600px and less than or equal to 992px. (This generally means tablet-width.)
  • Large—meaning greater than 992px and less than or equal to 1200px. (This generally means laptop or desktop-width.)

But what if you're reading this content on one of those ultra-wide desktop monitors? Good news! This content will span only 8 out of 12 columns when your window is very wide (greater than 1200px).

And what about mobile? Even better news! This content will span all 12 out of 12 columns when your window is narrow (less than 600px).

Test this responsive design by adjusting the width of your browser window!
